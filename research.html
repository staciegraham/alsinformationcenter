ALS Research & Clinical Trials

Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis (ALS) research is advancing rapidly, with scientists exploring new treatments, genetic factors, and potential cures. Clinical trials are essential in testing innovative therapies and improving patient care.

Current studies focus on slowing disease progression, improving motor function, and enhancing quality of life. Many trials are open for participation, offering hope to patients and families.
